Share Food Program Capstone Project
Philadelphia's Share Food Program was in need of a food pantry web map to serve its clients, but in order to keep it up to date, an effective data management strategy needed to be designed and implemented.

This system will centralize data storage, while increasing its security and usability in other capacities. Future updates will expand upon this system, which will eventually encompass location data from all of the organization's programs and distribution sites.
