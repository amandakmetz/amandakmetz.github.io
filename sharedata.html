Food Pantry Database
A SQLite database created to replace the flat files storing information about the Share Food Program's pantries and their activities.

The core of the new Share Food database is the pantry table. This will hold current locations and contact information for food pantries within the program's network.

To model the relationships between food pantries and their parent organizations appropriately, a host table was added to hold separate information for the broader organization that may operate several of Share's sites.

Food pantries update their information several times per year in the form of surveys, and those responses will be logged in the pantry_survey table. The responses from each new survey may be used to update the pantry table, but this table can be used to track all the surveys that a pantry has taken.

The pantry_delivery table will track all deliveries made by Share to a food pantry.
